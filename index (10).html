Chapter One

The Origin Narrative

Before it was a ticker, it was a bedtime story the internet told about itself.

"Tendies" — internet shorthand for chicken tenders — began as lore about a certain stereotypical man-child, still living in his mother's basement, whose entire economy ran on one currency: behavior.

In the story, he earns Good Boy Points for acceptable conduct — showering, leaving the room, not yelling at the television — and redeems them for a plate of chicken tenders, prepared by a long-suffering mother upstairs.

Somewhere along the way, traders adopted the bit. A green candle stopped being a "return on investment" and started being tendies. A red one meant no tendies tonight. It was never really about the money. It was about the plate at the end of a good day.
